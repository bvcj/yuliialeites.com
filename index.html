ABOUT

Yuliia Leites is a psychoanalytic psychotherapist from Kyiv, Ukraine, and a candidate of the International Psychoanalytical Association (IPA).

Her practice sits at the fracture points of war and social upheaval — with civilians, soldiers and queer people.

As a member of the Ukrainian Psychedelic Research Association (UPRA), she brings psychoanalytic thinking into psychedelic-assisted therapy — a radical method for a radical age.
